---
title: Heading
description: Astro Heading component for rendering h1-h6 tags
---
### Props
Extends [Tag](/utility/tag), [Children](/utility/children).

### Defaults
```ts
as = 'h2' 
contrast = true
```

## Examples
The examples below could also be written with [different syntax](/overview/syntax).

### Color
```astro live
---
import { Heading } from '@fullui/ui'
---

<Heading>The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog</Heading=>   
<Heading color="accent">The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog</Heading=>  
```

### Contrast
```astro live
---
import { Heading } from '@fullui/ui'
---

<Heading contrast={false}>The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog</Heading=>   
<Heading contrast={false} color="accent">The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog</Heading=>  
```

### Size
```astro live
---
import { Heading } from '@fullui/ui'
--- 
<Heading size="xl">The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog</Heading>   
<Heading size="lg">The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog</Heading>   
<Heading size="md">The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog</Heading>  
<Heading size="sm">The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog</Heading>   
<Heading size="xs">The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog</Heading>   
```

### Compact
Why size and compact? Read [the page about sizes](/theme/size).
```astro live
---
import { Heading } from '@fullui/ui'
--- 
<Heading size="xl" compact>The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog</Heading>   
<Heading size="lg" compact>The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog</Heading>   
<Heading size="md" compact>The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog</Heading>  
<Heading size="sm" compact>The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog</Heading>
<Heading size="xs" compact>The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog</Heading>   
```
