_layout: StructureLayout
title: Split
description: Structures children in equal columns above a certain breakpoint, based on CSS grid. This component sizes dynamically, the margin between a heading and a paragraph is smaller than the margin between a paragraph an image. On small screens it behaves the same as `column`.
---
